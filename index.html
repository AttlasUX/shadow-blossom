그림자 꽃

SHADOW BLOSSOM

기다림 WAITING

동굴 속 깊은 침묵,
천일의 인내로 빛을 얻다.

Deep silence in the cave,
gaining light through a thousand days of patience.

"La fuerza más grande no es el trueno, sino la montaña que espera el cambio de estación."

열정 PASSION

재 속에서 피어난 꽃,
끝없는 순환의 시작.

A flower blooming in ash,
the start of an endless cycle.

"The fire that survives the ash is the only one capable of forging the future."

흐름 FLOW

막힘없는 물의 길,
자유로운 형상의 노래.

The path of water without blockages,
a song of free form.

"To flow is not to surrender to the path, but to become the path itself."

뿌리 ROOT

대지를 딛는 굳은 발걸음,
하늘을 품은 거대한 힘.

Firm steps upon the earth,
great power embracing the sky.

"True strength is found in roots so deep that even the sky feels within reach."

그림자 SHADOW

빛이 강할수록 짙어지는,
가려진 진실의 조각.

Deepening as the light grows stronger,
a fragment of hidden truth.

"The deepest shadow is only a reflection of the light we have yet to understand."

속삭임 WHISPER

바람은 형체 없이 흐르고,
세상의 비밀을 실어 나른다.

Wind flows without a form,
carrying the secrets of the world.

"Wisdom does not arrive with thunder, but in the silence carried by the wind."
